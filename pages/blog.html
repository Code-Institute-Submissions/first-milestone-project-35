New course dates

Hi! It's been a busy few weeks lately with selling some of my sculptures at the local Christmas market. The interest seemed really big this year and we had more visitors than ever. Other than that I'm now done with the planning for the springs coming course dates for people who wants to learn the art of pottery making and sculpturing. There will be a course starting the first week of February, March and April, and also a master class for deeper learning in the middle of May. In a few days I will add more information and provide a link to the booking service.
